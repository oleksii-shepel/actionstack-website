Powerful State Management
ActionStack enables Angular developers to easily define, update, and access application state.
Motivation

Born from years of experience and designed to streamline state management, ActionStack tackles complexity by eliminating manual manipulation and seamlessly integrating async features into a unified pipeline. This ensures predictable state updates, even with concurrent operations.

Key Features

  • Reactive and declarative state management: ActionStack takes a modern and reactive approach to state management, leveraging RxJS observables to ensure your components instantly reflect the latest state. No more manual data synchronization needed! Components automatically receive updates, making development simpler and more efficient.

  • Powerful async handling: As applications grow, asynchronous operations can swiftly morph into a tangled mess of code. ActionStack swoops in with a powerful arsenal of creators to organize and control your async workflows. Craft custom solutions with its extensible system of strategies.

  • Transparent pipeline: Imagine a detailed blueprint visualizing every action's journey within your application. That's the power of ActionStack's transparent pipeline. See the origin of each action, understanding its context and purpose. Observe how side effects are triggered and managed, ensuring predictable outcomes and data consistency. See exactly how actions reshape your application's state.
